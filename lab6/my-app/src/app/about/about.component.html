About Korean Dramas

Popular Korean Dramas

My Love from the Star

A fantasy romance featuring an alien who has been living on Earth for 400 years and a top actress. The drama explores timeless love and the challenges of being from different worlds.

Descendants of the Sun

A military romance that follows the love story between a soldier and a doctor in a fictional war-torn country. The drama gained international fame for its gripping plot and charismatic leads.

Crash Landing on You

A romantic comedy-drama about a South Korean heiress who accidentally paraglides into North Korea and falls in love with a North Korean army officer. The series explores love overcoming political and cultural differences.
