Eternal Embrace

In love's soft dance, two hearts entwine,
A melody sweet, a love divine.
Through whispered words and gentle touch,
A symphony of emotions, oh, how they clutch.
In moonlit nights, where stars align,
Love's glow, a beacon, forever to shine.
Through joy and sorrow, a journey unfolds,
A story of passion, in love's script it's told.
Hand in hand, through the trials we face,
Love's embrace, a haven, a sacred space.
In laughter and tears, through thick and thin,
Love's resilient flame, a fire within.
